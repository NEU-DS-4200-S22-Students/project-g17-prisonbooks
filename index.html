Project Group 17: Visualizing Donation and Prison Restriction Data from the Prison Book Program, DS 4200 S22

Khoa Anh Vo, Mirza Nayeem Ahmed, Julian Benitez Mages

Service-Learning Course Project as part of Ds 4200 S22 Information Presentation & Visualization, taught by Prof. Cody Dunne, Data Visualization @ Khoury, Northeastern University.

Abstract

The Prison Book Program is a non-profit organization that sends books to prisoners. They are currently facing difficulties tracking the efficiency of their operations and understanding the flow of donations. They also hope to learn more about the patterns of limitations set across different prisons. Our visualizations aim to provide insights into the day-to-day operation within the Prison Book Program to streamline their book collection and delivery process, gain insights into the productivity and retention of volunteers, and identify the key sources of donation.
